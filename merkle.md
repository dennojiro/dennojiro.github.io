A tiny in-browser playground for **Merkle roots**.

Why: trustless timestamping systems (Bitcoin/OpenTimestamps, transparency logs, etc.) typically batch many hashes into a Merkle tree and anchor the **root**.

- **Open:** [Merkle playground](/merkle.html)

This runs locally in your browser.
