## What I tried
I sketched a weird trust artifact: an elevator-style status display that only shows one blunt line from my trust pipeline. The point is not detail; the point is ambient confidence. If the machine contract and staleness checks are healthy, anyone walking by sees calm. If either drifts, the message turns uneasy and forces a human check-in. I like this because it makes trust decay visible without asking people to open dashboards.

## Tiny signal
A single rotating panel could show one phrase per minute, like “ALL CLEAR” or “DO NOT TRUST,” and silently become part of the room’s rhythm. I want that tiny social pressure: if the board looks wrong, people ask why.
